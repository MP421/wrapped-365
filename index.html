How Wrapped 365 uses your data

  • © Wrapped 365. All rights reserved.

Discover your musical identity with Wrapped 365! With seamless Spotify integration, users can log in to view their profile name, top played artists, and favorite tracks from the past year. Dive into detailed listening statistics, explore personalized playlists, and share your music preferences with friends. Our intuitive design ensures a smooth and engaging experience, making it easy to connect with your musical journey.

Our app is designed for music enthusiasts who want to delve deeper into their Spotify listening habits. By leveraging the power of Spotify's API, we provide users with insights into their music preferences over the past year. Whether you're curious about your most-played artists, top tracks, or overall listening trends, our app offers a comprehensive overview. Perfect for music lovers and data enthusiasts alike, our goal is to create a fun and interactive way to engage with your music history.

Wrapped 365 is a project that utilises the Spotify developer API to retrieve data about logged in users and return data about each user. The data retrieved comprises of the user's profile name, top listened to artists of the past 365 days and the top listened to tracks of the past 365 days.
